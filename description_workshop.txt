[h1]Wu Dao Legends: A Wuxia Fantasy Mini Mod for Terraria[/h1]

[i]A fantasy wuxia experience inspired by Chinese martial arts mythology.[/i]

[hr]
[url=https://github.com/greenhand0403/WuDao]Source Code Repository[/url]

[b]Overview[/b]
This mod brings a unique wuxia-inspired fantasy experience to Terraria.  
A martial arts master from another world shattered the void and descended upon Terraria, altering parts of the world's rules and introducing brand-new mechanics and systems.

[hr]
[b]Main Features[/b]
[list]
[*][b]Ultimate Arts System[/b]: Equip martial techniques and consume Qi energy to unleash powerful wuxia-style abilities.
[*][b]Melee Blade Aura Visuals[/b]: Collect weapons and dyes to customize melee blade effects. Combine them for four different aura color variations.
[*][b]World Poison Mechanic[/b]: A progressive global debuff that intensifies as the game advances, increasing overall difficulty.
[*][b]Realm Progression & Scaling Weapons[/b]: Certain techniques, weapons, and accessories grow stronger as bosses are defeated. Includes scaling weapons and throwing-class dart weapons.
[*][b]Five Elements Armor System[/b]: Armor pieces possess elemental attributes. Matching elements grant additional bonuses.
[*][b]Cuisine & Cooking System[/b]: Tasting or crafting food for the first time increases Deliciousness and Cooking Skill values, providing bonuses to certain weapons and accessories.
[*][b]InvisibleEnemies: Configurable echo stealth effects for enemies, barrages, and trap blocks
[/list]

[h1]武道传说：泰拉瑞亚奇幻武侠小型模组[/h1]

[hr]
[url=https://github.com/greenhand0403/WuDao]代码仓库[/url]
[b]模组简介[/b]
本模组以奇幻武侠世界为核心，讲述异世界武道强者破碎虚空，降临泰拉瑞亚，改变了世界的部分规则，引入诸多新颖内容和机制

[hr]
[b]主要特色[/b]
[list]
[*]绝学系统：装备绝学，消耗气力值，释放武侠绝技
[*]近战刀光视觉：通过收藏武器，收藏染料，两两组合，有 4 种染色搭配
[*]世界之毒：随游戏进度递增的属性惩罚机制，增加游戏难度
[*]境界加成和成长型武器：部分绝学、武器和饰品随游戏进度增强，增添了部分随进度成长的武器，启用飞镖类武器造成投掷伤害
[*]盔甲五行：盔甲部件具有额外五行属性，提供额外加成效果
[*]厨艺和美食系统：首次品尝和制作食物，提升美味值和厨艺值，部分武器和饰品可获得额外的加成
[*]隐身敌怪：可配置敌怪、弹幕和陷阱物块的回声隐身效果
[/list]
