[h1]Wu Dao Legends: A Wuxia Fantasy Mini Mod for Terraria[/h1]

[i]A fantasy wuxia experience inspired by Chinese martial arts mythology.[/i]

[hr]
[url=https://github.com/greenhand0403/WuDao]Source Code Repository[/url]

[b]Overview[/b]  
This mod brings a unique wuxia-inspired fantasy experience to Terraria.  
A martial arts master from another world shattered the void and descended upon Terraria, altering parts of the world's rules and introducing brand-new mechanics and systems.

[hr]
[b]Main Features[/b]
[list]
[*][b]Ultimate Arts System[/b]: Equip martial techniques and consume Qi energy to unleash powerful wuxia-style abilities.  
[*][b]Melee Blade Aura Visuals[/b]: Collect weapons and dyes to customize melee blade effects. Combine them for four color variations.  
[*][b]Heaven Backlash[/b]: A progressive global debuff that intensifies as the game advances.  
[*][b]Martial Realm & Scaling Weapons[/b]: Certain techniques, weapons, and accessories grow stronger as bosses are defeated. Includes scaling weapons and throwing-class dart weapons.  
[*][b]Five Elements Armor System[/b]: Armor pieces possess elemental attributes. Matching elements grant additional bonuses.  
[*][b]Cuisine & Cooking System[/b]: Tasting or crafting food for the first time increases Deliciousness and Cooking Skill values, providing bonuses to certain weapons and accessories.  
[*][b]InvisibleEnemies[/b]: Configurable echo stealth effects for enemies, barrages, and trap blocks.  
[/list]
