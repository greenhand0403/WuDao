Wu Dao Legends is a wuxia-themed mod with a strong personal style.

A fantasy wuxia experience inspired by Chinese martial arts mythology.

A martial arts master from another world shattered the void and descended upon Terraria, altering parts of its rules and introducing new items and mechanics.

Core Features:

- Ultimate Arts System: Consume Qi energy to unleash unique martial abilities.
- Melee Blade Aura Visuals: Sword-type weapons feature customizable blade effects. Collect weapons and dyes to create four possible color combinations.
- World Poison: “The endlessly resurrecting player is the true corruption of the world.” A progressive global penalty that increases as the game advances.
- Realm Progression: Ultimate Arts damage and certain weapons and accessories scale with boss progression. Includes scaling weapons and dart-style throwing weapons.
- Five Elements Armor: Armor pieces possess elemental attributes. Matching elements grant additional bonuses.
- Deliciousness & Cooking Skill: Tasting or crafting food for the first time increases attributes that enhance certain weapons and accessories.

The mod is still in development. More wuxia-themed content and unique systems will be added in future updates.

这是一个充满个人风格的武侠主题模组。故事起因于异世界的武道强者破碎虚空，穿越到泰拉瑞亚，影响了世界的部分规则，并带来了全新物品与机制。

核心机制包括：

- 绝学系统：通过消耗气力值释放独特绝技。
- 近战剑类武器自带刀光染色视觉（收藏武器可以使用武器贴图刀光），还可以收藏背包中的首个染料进行染色，两两组合有四种刀光。
- 世界之毒：“不断重生的玩家才是泰拉世界的污染源”，随游戏进度逐步增强的世界性惩罚。
- 境界机制：“绝学伤害、部分武器和饰品的效果随击败 BOSS 进度逐渐增强。同时模组也有成长型武器、飞镖等投掷类武器。
- 五行盔甲：盔甲部件具有五行属性，同属性获得额外效果。
- 美味值与厨艺值：首次品尝或制作食物可提升相应属性，对部分武器和饰品有加成。
